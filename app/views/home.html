SELU ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery is an international organization for computing professionals. Southeastern's chapter of the ACM is focused on connecting Computer Science majors to invaluable resources to further their academic careers.

Faculty Sponsor

Dr. Kuo-pao Yang is the faculty sponsor for the SELU chapter of the ACM. His focus is providing a FUN learning environment for his students. Whether it is having them showing off their skills in their favorite programming language or building a robot to take over the world, his classes always intrigue.


"There are two types of learning, happy learning or suffering learning. I try to provide the happy learning, but if you are suffering you are still learning."
- Dr.Kuo-pao Yang
